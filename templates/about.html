About Me

This is what I do.

With over a decade of experience in the field of IT and infrastructure management, I have a proven track record of successfully handling various roles and responsibilities across different organizations. My career journey has equipped me with a diverse skill set and a deep understanding of modern technology stacks

I spent five years at Innroad, Inc., based in New York City, where I served as a DevOps Engineer and later as a Senior DevOps Engineer. During this time, I honed my expertise in cloud infrastructure management, primarily on Amazon AWS. I efficiently managed AWS resources using Terraform, ensuring the secure and optimized operation of various services such as EC2, RDS, IAM, Route53, ELB/ALB, S3, Lambda, OpenSearch/Elasticsearch/Kibana, Kubernetes, Docker, and Vault.

I played a crucial role in streamlining deployment processes by implementing automation pipelines using Jenkins. This involved creating CI/CD pipelines and writing Groovy scripts to ensure the rapid and reliable delivery of software applications.

I was responsible for maintaining version control systems using Bitbucket and GitHub, ensuring the integrity and traceability of code changes. Additionally, I managed critical tools and services like MS AD, JFrog, OneLogin, Duo, Datadog, Grafana, and PRTG Monitoring to monitor and optimize the company's infrastructure.

My role also included troubleshooting distributed applications, where I gained valuable experience in identifying and resolving complex technical issues. In terms of technology stack, I worked with a range of programming languages, including C#, NodeJS, JavaScript, and Python.

Throughout my career, I have continuously adapted to emerging technologies and demonstrated a commitment to ensuring the stability, security, and efficiency of IT infrastructure. My diverse skill set, combined with my problem-solving abilities, makes me a valuable asset in complex and dynamic IT environments.
